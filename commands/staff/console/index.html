Hello Staff.

Provided are commands you can execute from discord

Commands:

  • !gameban [RobloxUsername/RobloxUserId] [time] [reason]
  • !gamekick [RobloxUsername/RobloxUserId] [reason]

You can copy the first command here as an example

!gamekick RuleBreaker 1 1d Bye!

Time Formatting

  • 1s = 1 Second
  • 60s = 1 Minute

    • Note: for minutes of time you want to do it in seconds. 5 minutes is 360 seconds

  • 1h = 1 Hour
  • 1d = 1 Day
  • 1w = 1 Week
  • 1m = 1 Month
  • 1y = 1 Year
  • 1k = Permanent
